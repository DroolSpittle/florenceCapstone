Short Description of the Capstone

This capstone will serve to educate myself and others about wine culture in the Chianti region. This will include using a few wines as examples as well as explaining the production of wines in the Chianti region. This will expand upon wine knowledge acquired before coming to Florence Italy.

History

The history page will describe the history of the Chianti wine, as well as describe the differences between what classifies as a chianti and a chainti classico, two different designations of wine.

Wines

The wines page will include two wines that are made in the Chianti region. This will include brief descriptions of their flavors and will branch to individual pages that further describe the wines. These descriptions will also include the pairings of each wine, as well as what grapes are used to make them.
This section will also include a list of other popular wines in the region as well as short descriptions of those.

Production

The production page will briefly describe how wine is produced in the Chianti region, starting from the growing of the grapes, to the bottling. This section will also include a list of other grapes grown in the region and a short description of those. Also there will be a list of the different effects of weather on the grapes.
